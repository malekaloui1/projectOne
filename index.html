let's discover our history

Tunisian history is a rich tapestry woven with the threads of ancient civilizations and diverse cultural influences. Located on the Mediterranean coast of North Africa, Tunisia's history dates back millennia. It was once inhabited by indigenous Berber tribes before becoming a part of Phoenician Carthage, a formidable maritime power in antiquity. The Punic Wars between Carthage and Rome left an indelible mark, leading to the Roman conquest of Tunisia. Subsequent centuries saw Byzantine, Arab, and Ottoman rule, each contributing to the country's cultural mosaic. In the 20th century, Tunisia played a pivotal role in the Arab Spring, leading to its transition to a democratic republic. Today, Tunisia's history is a testament to its resilience, embracing its ancient heritage while forging a path towards modernity and democratic governance.
